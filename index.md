## 💡 Frequently Asked Questions

Can I run this on a laptop?
Yes, as long as you maintain a stable internet connection.

What happens if I turn off my computer?
The software pauses all active trades. When you restart the application, you must initiate the strategy again.

Does the software charge a commission? 
The software operates based on your own settings. Fees related to blockchain transactions depend on the current network conditions, not the application itself.

How do I remove the software?
Open your Windows Control Panel, select "Add or Remove Programs," and choose the software from the list.

What if the application crashes?
Restart the application. The internal logs will help you identify where the process stopped.

Is my private key stored on a server?
No. All sensitive keys remain on your computer. The software does not transmit your private keys to remote servers.

Should I use this with large amounts of money?
Start with small amounts to understand how the AI reacts to market changes. Increase your allocation once you feel comfortable with the software performance.

Do I need to manage gas fees?
The AI handles fee calculations to ensure your transactions execute under expected conditions. You can adjust the "Gas Priority" setting in the dashboard if you want faster transaction times.

Can I move my funds out at any time?
Yes. Use the "Withdraw" button in the wallet tab to move funds to an exchange or a cold hardware wallet at any point.
